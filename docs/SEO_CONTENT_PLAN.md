# SEO Content Plan for VPP Finder

This document outlines blog content and landing pages to drive organic traffic from Australian battery owners searching for VPP information.

---

## High-Intent Keywords (Bottom of Funnel)

These users are ready to join a VPP. Create dedicated landing pages or detailed guides.

### 1. "Best VPP for Tesla Powerwall 2025"
**Search Volume:** ~500/month (estimated)
**Competition:** Medium
**Intent:** Transactional

**Content Outline:**
- H1: Best VPP Programs for Tesla Powerwall in 2025
- Introduction: Why Tesla Powerwall owners have great VPP options
- Quick answer: Top 3 picks (Amber, AGL, Origin)
- Comparison table: All Tesla-compatible VPPs
- Detailed breakdown of each program
- How to choose based on your priorities (earnings vs simplicity vs control)
- FAQ section
- CTA: Use our AI matcher to get personalized recommendations

**Target URL:** /best-vpp-tesla-powerwall
